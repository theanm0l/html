Android Versions

  1. Android 1.0 (2008)

    Android 1.0, the first commercial version of the software, was released on September 23, 2008. It included features such as a home screen and contact list, and it allowed users to download third-party apps.

  2. Android 1.5 Cupcake (2009)

    Android 1.5 Cupcake, a minor update to the original version, was released on April 27, 2009. It introduced new features such as an on-screen keyboard and support for third-party virtual keyboards.

  3. Android 2.0/2.1 Eclair (2009)

    Android 2.0/2.1 Eclair, a major update to Cupcake, was released on October 26, 2009. It introduced new features such as Google Maps Navigation and support for HTML5 in the browser.

  4. Android 2.2 Froyo (2010)

    Android 2.2 Froyo, short for "frozen yogurt," was released on May 20, 2010. It introduced new features such as support for Adobe Flash in the browser and improved performance through the JIT compiler.

  5. Android 2.3 Gingerbread (2010)

    Android 2.3 Gingerbread, named after a popular cookie, was released on December 6, 2010. It introduced new features such as support for Near Field Communication (NFC) and improved gaming performance.
